About Me

Hello! I am Bhaskar B Kidiyoor, a passionate artist and painter dedicated to creating beautiful and functional art pieces. With years of experience in various art forms, I take pride in my ability to transform ideas into stunning visual representations.

My artistic journey began at a young age, and I have continually refined my skills in different mediums, from oil painting to digital art. I believe that art is not just a form of expression but also a means to connect with others. Each piece I create tells a story, reflecting the emotions and experiences I encounter in life.

I enjoy collaborating with clients to bring their visions to life, whether it’s through custom paintings, unique signage, or intricate engravings. My goal is to create art that resonates and makes an impact, enhancing the spaces and lives of those who experience it.

Explore my portfolio to see my work and learn more about my services. I look forward to sharing my passion for art with you!

My Skills

With a passion for creativity and a keen eye for detail, I specialize in a wide array of artistic services. My expertise includes creating stunning digital boards, eye-catching sign boards, and intricate computer sticker cuttings. I excel in crafting unique foam letters, vibrant cloth banners, and professional digital banners that elevate any space. My skills extend to granite engraving, designing front lite boards, and producing custom number plates. As an accomplished oil painter, I bring life to canvases and interiors with my interior arts. Furthermore, I offer services in screen/offset printing, rubber stamps, and creating detailed clay models and plaster arts. My craftsmanship includes plastic name boards, steel letters, and elegant brass name boards. Whether it's t-shirt printing or LED boards, I am dedicated to delivering exceptional quality and creativity in every project, ensuring that each piece resonates with beauty and purpose.
